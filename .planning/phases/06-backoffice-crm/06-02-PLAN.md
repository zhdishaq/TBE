Reconciliation discrepancy types:
- `OrphanStripeEvent`: StripeEvent with no matching WalletTransaction (by PaymentIntentId) → High severity
- `OrphanWalletRow`: WalletTransaction claiming a PaymentIntentId that has no corresponding StripeEvent → High severity
- `AmountDrift`: Matched pair where `StripeEvent.amount != |WalletTransaction.SignedAmount| * 100` (Stripe in pence) — ≤£5 drift → Low, >£5 → High
- `UnprocessedEvent`: StripeEvent where `Processed=0` for more than 1 hour → High severity
