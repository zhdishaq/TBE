<objective>
Ship car hire + transfer search surfaces, a car voucher email pipeline, and the full Trip Builder (flight + hotel in one basket with a combined checkout). This plan is the capstone of Phase 4 — it closes all remaining phase requirements (CARB-01..03, PKG-01..04) by composing primitives from 04-00/01/02/03.

**Committed Stripe strategy — single combined PaymentIntent with sequential partial captures (per CONTEXT D-08, D-10):**
- ONE PaymentIntent per basket, created with `capture_method: "manual"` and amount = basket total, with deterministic idempotency key `basket-{basketId}-authorize`.
- Customer sees ONE Stripe PaymentElement and authorizes ONE amount.
- Customer's statement shows ONE charge for the basket total (D-08: "One charge on the customer's statement").
- Capture is event-driven in two stages mapped to saga outcomes (D-10):
  1. On `FlightTicketed`: `PaymentIntent.Capture` with `PaymentIntentCaptureOptions { AmountToCapture = flightSubtotal, FinalCapture = false }` — this captures the flight portion but keeps the remaining authorization alive.
  2. On `HotelBookingConfirmed` (full success): `PaymentIntent.Capture` with `PaymentIntentCaptureOptions { AmountToCapture = hotelSubtotal, FinalCapture = true }` — this captures the hotel portion and closes the PI.
- Partial failure (D-09): on `HotelBookingFailed` after flight is already captured, orchestrator calls `PaymentIntent.Capture` with `AmountToCapture = 0, FinalCapture = true` (or the Stripe-documented equivalent for releasing remainder) so only the flight portion is charged; the hotel subtotal is released from the hold. Customer sees the success (flight charge on statement), the refund/release (hotel subtotal not charged), and receives a single email per D-09.
- Hard failure before any capture (both legs fail, or customer abandons): void the single PI — no charges on statement.

Purpose: Customers can book a combined flight+hotel in a single checkout with one charge and clean partial-failure behavior, and can book car hire / transfers.
Output: Full Phase 4 B2C portal feature set is shippable after this plan.
</objective>
